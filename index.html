Inside the book

The book

For the moment when you do not know what you feel yet.

Sit With It is a 66-page guided interior for people who can feel something moving, but do not yet have the words for it. It is not a book about fixing yourself faster. It is about making contact with what is here until it becomes gentler, clearer, or ready to move.

The finished interior moves from a gifting page and soft introduction through four states, each with an opener, grounding ritual, original prompts, affirmation, and tear-out page. It closes with notes pages and a quiet ending.

Paperback 6 x 9 in Black ink Cream paper No bleed

Why it works

Psychology, kept soft enough to use.

The book is built around a simple human problem: often we do not know what we feel until language gives us a place to begin. The prompts gently build emotional granularity, then ask what happened just before, where it lives in the body, and what kind of care might be possible now.

Under the surface are familiar ideas from affect labelling, the window of tolerance, ACT, and self-compassion. On the page, they stay plain: name the weather, come back to the body, speak to yourself like someone you love.
